1- Dry Hair: It is the hair that immediately becomes dry after washing and loses its shine very quickly due to the lack of oils secretions.

2- Oily Hair: It is hair that appears greasy as a result of increased sebaceous secretions from the glands in the scalp and skin.

3- Normal Hair: It is considered between (oily and dry), so it is not very shiny and at the same time it is not dry, as there is a balance in the amount of oils produced by the scalp.

4- Mixed Hair: It is a mixture of the previous types. We find that the scalp (hair roots) itself is oily in the upper part and dry in the ends of the hair. It is the most difficult type of hair to deal with, as it requires special treatment and extreme care.
